Discover Oslo

Oslo, the capital of Norway, is known for its green spaces and museums. The city is situated on the country's southern coast at the head of the Oslofjord. Oslo is also known for its modern architecture, restaurants, and the beautiful natural surroundings which include hills, forests, and lakes.

Key attractions include the Vigeland Sculpture Park, the Viking Ship Museum, the Oslo Opera House, and the Royal Palace. Whether you're interested in outdoor activities, cultural experiences, or culinary delights, Oslo has something to offer everyone.
