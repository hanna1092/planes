Meals & Drinks

Enjoy Delicious Meals and Drinks

On our flights, you have the option to order meals to suit your preferences. Whether you're in the mood for something savory or sweet, we have a variety of meal options available.

Meals

Our meals are carefully prepared to cater to a variety of tastes and dietary preferences. Whether you prefer a hearty main course or a lighter option, our menu offers a selection that ensures a delightful dining experience. We also accommodate special dietary needs upon request, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a satisfying meal during their flight.

Selection of Snacks

Throughout the flight, you can also choose from a selection of snacks to satisfy your cravings. From healthy options like fresh fruits and nuts to indulgent treats such as chocolates and cookies, there's something to suit every taste.

Choice of Beverages

Our beverage selection includes soft drinks, juices, tea, coffee, and alcoholic beverages. You can enjoy these beverages either to complement your meal or to refresh yourself at any time during the flight. We offer a variety of options, from carbonated drinks and natural juices to a selection of teas and freshly brewed coffee.

No matter if you're looking for something sweet or savory, we have something for you. Our onboard menu offers a wide range of choices, ensuring everyone finds something enjoyable for their journey.
