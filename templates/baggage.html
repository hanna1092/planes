Baggage

Hand Baggage

Here you will find all necessary information regarding carry-on baggage. Hand baggage is the luggage you can take with you into the aircraft cabin. Below are details about its dimensions, weight, and regulations.

  • Dimensions: Typically up to 55 x 40 x 23 cm.
  • Weight: Usually up to 8-10 kg.
  • Placement: Must fit in the overhead compartment or under the seat in front of you.
  • Prohibited items: Sharp objects, liquids over a certain volume, etc., are not allowed in carry-on baggage.
  • Additional information: Items such as laptops, cosmetics, etc., may have specific requirements.

Checked Baggage

Here you will find all necessary information regarding checked baggage. Checked baggage is transported in the aircraft's hold and can be purchased in addition to carry-on baggage.

  • Dimensions: Typically up to 158 cm (total length + width + height).
  • Weight: Depending on the airline, typically up to 20-23 kg per piece of luggage.
  • Fees: Airlines often charge fees for checked baggage.
  • Prohibited items: Similar to carry-on baggage, there are restrictions on certain items.
  • Additional information: Special procedures may be required during check-in and baggage claim.
