Travel with Pets

Take your pet along on an unforgettable journey!

Key Information

Traveling with pets can be a wonderful experience. It's important to ensure your pet's comfort and safety throughout the journey. Whether it's a dog, cat, or another small animal, we have guidelines to make traveling with your pet hassle-free.

Permitted Pets

We allow various types of pets on board, including dogs, cats, rabbits, and small birds. Each type of pet may have specific regulations and requirements for travel.

Required Documents

When traveling with your pet, certain documents are necessary, such as health certificates, vaccination records, and import/export permits. Ensure you have all required paperwork ready before your journey to avoid any delays.

Pet Containers

For the comfort and safety of your pet, we provide pet containers that meet airline regulations. These containers are designed to ensure your pet's well-being during the flight.
